Fifty Shades of Shay

A lifestyle blog about living with mental and neurological disabilities

The vision for this blog is to provide insights and suggestions for people living with neurological and mental disabilities that help to enhance their quality of life.

The posts, tips, articles, and suggestions are not meant to replace traditional therapy and prescribed medications. However; it is meant to complement both, and aid in increasing self sufficiency.
